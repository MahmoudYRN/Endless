Endless Guides!!

This guides brings to you, things you could do to make your computer perform smoothly and last longer

Keeping up-to-date

Kepeing your computer up-to-date is one wya you can ensure both your software and hardware components are up to date, by doing this you're allowing your device to perform at its optimum state. Here at Endless we recommend all of our customers run their operating system on a solid-state drive which will result in their devices running much faster and quieter and most importantly helps it last longer. Below are steps which shows you how to keep your device up-to-date:

  1. Bring up the start menu, then navigate to Settings > Update & security > Windows Update.

  2. Select Check for updates.
  3. If updates are avaliable, you will be able to install them at any given time.

Clean up your mess!!

Everytime your computer is being accessed temporary cache files are created to store important files incase of an emermgency. Once you're done with this, usually the temporary files are deleted but in some cases the files stay in a folder which is called "Temp" this is where you will find all the unnessucary file that are taking up space and affecting the performance of your computer. To get rid of temporary files do the following:

  1. Press Windows + R and search up "temp" in the presented dialog

  2. It may ask for administrator permission, just continue with it
  3. Highlight all the files and delete them
  4. Now you can restart your device and should see an improvement

Keep out Viruses

Downloading alot of files from the internet can introduce viruses into your computer sometimes without you knowing if the site isnt trusted, once a viruses has interred your device it can do many things such as wipe some of your important files, or eat up memory by multiplying and spreading around the computer which will decrease the performance signifcantly. To get rid of the viruses and improve the performance, do the following:

  1. Press windows button for the start menu then search "Virus & threat protection"

  2. In the application scan for viruses this shouldnt take long
  3. If a virus or a threat has be identified you can then wipe them
  4. Now you can restart your device and use it as normal

Defrag!

You may have heard of the term "defragmentation", this is similar to the removal of temeprary files we discussed above, however this is a higher type of maintenance which is done to keep your computer running smoothyl. Defragmentatiton should be done at least once every month to keep out unwanted files and healither storage device. To defrag your computer follow these steps:

  1. Press windows button for the start menu then search "defragment and optimise drivers"

  2. Select the storage device and click "optimise"
  3. Once the process is done you can then restart your computer
  4. Now you can enjoy your new and faster computer
